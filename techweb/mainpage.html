About our club

TechPlay is a club that brings together gaming and programming. It approaches gaming as more than entertainment—treating it as a way to build strategic thinking, analytical skills, and teamwork.

Our club helps members build strong computational thinking skills and learn programming languages, while also improving problem-solving, creativity, and teamwork with support from experienced mentors and teachers.

Our environment

  • Spacious main room with a small snack corner.
  • Gaming PCs or laptops available
  • One or two consoles (Xbox or Nintendo Switch)
  • Large screen or projector for group activities
  • Tables and chairs arranged for teamwork
  • Strong, reliable internet connection
  • Whiteboard or bulletin board for planning and lessons
  • Budget-friendly accessories (headphones, keyboards, extension cords, surge protectors)
  • Simple decorations with gaming and coding posters

Skills that you improve in "TechPlay"

  • Advanced problem-solving abilities.
  • Computational thinking and logical reasoning.
  • Creativity and originality .
  • Improved teamwork and leadership.
  • Communication skills .
  • Time management
  • Technical skills
  • Programming skills
